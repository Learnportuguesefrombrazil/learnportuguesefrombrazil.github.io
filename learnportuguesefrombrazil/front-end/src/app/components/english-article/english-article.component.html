Survey Reveals Most Common Daily Mishaps

Sometimes our mistakes can have serious consequences. But a lot of the time, the mistakes we make in daily life are just a little annoying. We often call these little mistakes "mishaps."

And survey company OnePoll has been asking people about their daily mishaps. After talking to 2,000 people, the company made a list of 20 of the most common mishaps. For example, 11% of people surveyed said they had dropped a book while reading in the bath.

Other mishaps on the list include getting on the wrong train or bus, sending a text message to the wrong person, and making a meal for work but accidentally leaving it at home.

Sixteen percent of people admitted they had forgotten about plans they had made, while others said they had greeted someone they thought they knew, but then realized they did not actually know the person.

According to the survey, the average adult has 84 mishaps every year ‚Äî more than one each week. And while 21% of people said they had learned something from their mistakes, 31% said that they had made the same mistake again.
